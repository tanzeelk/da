About the group

Founded in 1938 by Hanuman Prasad Agarwal, DA Group’s journey began as a humble cotton manufacturing unit. Now, more than 80 years later, we have expanded beyond our roots, delving into sectors such as textiles, infrastructure, childcare initiatives, and impactful CSR activities. As one of India’s largest and most esteemed third-generation business conglomerates, our journey reflects the commitment towards driving positive change within the community while keeping up with the ever-changing market trends. As we continue contributing to India’s economic growth, we are poised to playing a key role in inspiring and supporting the youth of our nation, striving to enhance their prospects and opportunities.

Vision

Our goal is to equip the young workforce across the nation to achieve their potential. By helping people with the resources and opportunities they need, we aim to build an empowered society.

Mission

Our approach is rooted in a passion for collective betterment that retains family values. Through a blend of social and economic ethics, we aim for excellence in every endeavor we undertake.

Values

Everything we do for economic and social betterment is based on four core values. Integrity, Commitment, Excellence and Innovation drive every facet of our business, guiding our efforts towards success.
