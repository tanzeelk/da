About the group

Founded in 1938 by Hanuman Prasad Agarwal, DA Group’s journey began as a humble cotton manufacturing unit. Now, more than 80 years later, we have expanded beyond our roots, delving into sectors such as textiles, infrastructure, childcare initiatives, and impactful CSR activities. As one of India’s largest and most esteemed third-generation business conglomerates, our journey reflects the commitment towards driving positive change within the community while keeping up with the ever-changing market trends. As we continue contributing to India’s economic growth, we are poised to playing a key role in inspiring and supporting the youth of our nation, striving to enhance their prospects and opportunities.

Vision

Our goal is to equip the young workforce across the nation to achieve their potential. By helping people with the resources and opportunities they need, we aim to build an empowered society.

Mission

Our approach is rooted in a passion for collective betterment that retains family values. Through a blend of social and economic ethics, we aim for excellence in every endeavor we undertake.

Values

Everything we do for economic and social betterment is based on four core values. Integrity, Commitment, Excellence and Innovation drive every facet of our business, guiding our efforts towards success.

Our founders

DA Group was founded by Anil Agarwal, a pioneer in India's raw cotton trading industry. His guiding principle "Be excellent at what you do, and when you've achieved that, give back to society and pass on the torch," shapes his approach towards business and life. Under his leadership, the flagship firm Durgesh Impex Pvt. Ltd. evolved into one of the most advanced integrated textile units in the country.

Anil Agarwal
Durgesh Agarwal

A dynamic entrepreneur from India, Durgesh Agarwal carried the family business forward while maintaining strong family values and the commitment to ethical business practices. His dedication towards excellence has driven him since the very beginning of his career, reflecting through his approach of “zero tolerance” towards any compromise in quality.

An educationist, philanthropist and social entrepreneur, Usha Agarwal has dedicated her life to creating sustainable change at the grassroots level. Guided by the principle "भिक्षा नहीं शिक्षा" (Support education, not begging), she founded Shree Shakti Seva Kendra with a vision to empower underserved communities through education, women's development, and livelihood opportunities.

Usha Agarwal
Kanica Agarwal

Recognising the need for safe, engaging, and educational play experiences for children in Ahmedabad, Kanica Agarwal founded Toy Joy Tales. Inspired by her own journey as a mother of two, she envisioned a space where children could play, learn, and grow through mindful exploration. Under her leadership, Toy Joy Tales has evolved into a thoughtfully curated destination that nurtures creativity, curiosity, and holistic early childhood development.

Our Progress

Hanuman Prasad Agarwal founded the cotton business under the Eastern Group in Bengal, which expanded to Rajasthan, Punjab and Tamil Nadu over the next 40 years.

1938
1978

The business was relocated to Gujarat by Anil Agarwal, continuing the trading of raw cotton.

With plans in place for expansion to other endeavours, the business strengthened its position in Gujarat.

1982
1989

The cotton business flourished under the leadership of Anil Agarwal, establishing a nationwide trading network.

A fully automated ginning mill spanning approximately five acres was set up in Ahmedabad, Gujarat.

1990
1997

Another ginning plant was inaugurated in Himmatnagar, Gujarat.

With a focus on modernisation, Durgesh Agarwal entered the family business, rebranding the group as Durgesh Impex.

2004
2008

The real estate business was streamlined by Durgesh Agarwal, with a freshly structured approach and a long-term vision.

A cotton manufacturing unit was established in Jalgaon, Maharashtra. Inspired by Usha Agarwal's vision, the family business also expanded into pre-primary education by establishing Ahmedabad's Holistic Childcare Centre.

2009
2015

Numerous key real estate projects were completed.

Marking our entry into the events and entertainment sector, Shree Shakti Convention Centre (SSCC) was launched, creating a landmark destination for large-scale conventions, celebrations and corporate gatherings.

2020
2021
